Essential Health Tips

Maximize the benefits of your organic diet with these hypertension-friendly lifestyle practices.

Hydration

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration helps maintain blood volume and supports kidney function.

Sodium Awareness

Limit sodium intake to less than 1500mg per day. Avoid processed foods and use herbs for flavoring.

Regular Exercise

Aim for 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily. Walking, swimming, and cycling are excellent choices.

Stress Management

Practice mindfulness, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to help lower blood pressure naturally.
